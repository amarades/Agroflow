# AgroFlow AI - Hackathon Pitch

## 🎤 30-Second Elevator Pitch

"Farmers lose nearly 30% of their income due to inefficient distribution and middlemen. **AgroFlow AI** changes this. We connect farmers directly to buyers using an intelligent optimization algorithm that considers price, distance, demand, and spoilage risk. The result? Maximum profit for farmers, reduced waste, and fair pricing for all."

---

## 📝 Full Presentation (3 Minutes)

### 1. The Problem (30 seconds)

"Currently in India, farmers face three major challenges:
- They don't know WHERE demand is highest
- They don't know WHAT price maximizes profit
- They don't know WHICH buyer minimizes distribution costs

**This results in 20-30% income loss and massive crop wastage.**"

---

### 2. Our Solution (45 seconds)

"**AgroFlow AI** is a smart platform that:
- Lets farmers list their crops with quantity and pricing
- Allows buyers to post demands with their requirements
- Uses an **AI optimization engine** to match the best buyer-farmer pairs

The system analyzes FOUR key factors in real-time:
1. **Price Margin** - What's the profit?
2. **Distance** - What's the transport cost?
3. **Demand** - Who needs it most urgently?
4. **Spoilage Risk** - Which crops expire soon?

And recommends the **optimal match** that maximizes farmer profit."

---

### 3. Technical Innovation (45 seconds)

"Our competitive advantage is the **scoring algorithm**:

```
Score = Price Margin - Transport Cost + Demand Priority - Spoilage Penalty
```

Every potential match gets a score. The highest score wins.

**Example:**
- Farmer Ravi grows 100kg tomatoes at ₹20/kg
- Buyer A offers ₹30/kg but is 50km away → Score: +2
- Buyer B offers ₹28/kg but is 10km away → Score: +15
- **System recommends Buyer B** → Ravi earns MORE after transport costs

This is smart, data-driven decision making."

---

### 4. Real-World Impact (30 seconds)

"This system can be deployed TODAY using:
- Real-time **mandi price APIs**
- **Google Maps** for actual routes
- **SMS notifications** for rural farmers without smartphones

It's scalable, practical, and directly addresses government initiatives for farmer welfare."

---

Simple. Intuitive. Powerful."
